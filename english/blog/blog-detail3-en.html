Benefits of dentals implants

  • Home
  • Blog

Dentistry has a wide range of materials with which to work, which provides many options to choose from during treatment. when they developed Dental implants, the only material available was titanium.
Although it has proven to be an excellent solution for restoring teeth missing, this material still has some flaws that made the scientists and researchers looked for a different option, which resulted in the creation of Zirconia implants.
Zirconia is a resistant material capable of covering the defects that the titanium, becoming a great alternative while thinking about what kind of implant done. In this article, we will explain what zirconia implants are and its many benefits.

What are its benefits?

Zirconia implants have many properties that make them unique and efficient, such as color and resistance. However, its main benefit comes from its biocompatibility.

Biocompatibility

This is the property that allows materials to be used within the body without be harmful or produce rejection or allergies.
As zirconia is a ceramic material free of metallic particles, it becomes in a highly biocompatible alternative. Therefore, limiting the chances of rejection and eliminating the risk of metal allergies that could compromise the integrity of the treatment.
In addition, this allows for better healing after placement surgery. implant and adequate integration between it and the bone.
In addition, its chemical composition attracts fewer bacteria to its environment, which makes it less prone to infections.

Strength

As the human mouth is a complex system with many forces being applied constantly in different directions, the implant must be sufficiently strong enough to hold them smoothly. Zirconia is perfectly capable of resist these forces, allowing you to eat freely without the risk of breaking or splinter.

Colour

Another essential aspect of Zirconia is its optical properties. This material has a white color, similar to that of a natural tooth.
This allows its use without the risk of developing gum stains that could lead to unpleasant looks. Also, in thin and translucent gums, the White Zirconia color gives a natural look without compromising aesthetics.

In conclusion, zirconia has proven to be an excellent alternative as a material of implant. As it is highly biocompatible, it can be used to ensure that rejection of the implant does not occur. In addition, its metal-free characteristic it becomes the best holistic solution to restore missing teeth. For Therefore, zirconia implants will help you get the results you are looking for. more safely and securely, without compromising your appearance.
