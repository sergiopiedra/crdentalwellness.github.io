Guanacaste, its main attraction in Costa Rica

  • Inicio
  • Blog

Once you have decided to travel to Costa Rica, whether for dental tourism or simply as a regular visitor, you have the task of choosing your stops and destinations. And, although it seems like a simple matter, you may be overwhelmed by all the options in this heavenly place. Besides all the amazing beaches, sun and heat and flavors of the Caribbean, you should consider Guanacaste as one of your best options to visit.
Why choose Guanacaste as a destination? Costa Rica has many tourist attractions. And, since Punta Cana and Cancun are the top two destinations among foreigners, you may be wondering why choose anywhere else.
All on one On the one hand, while Punta Cana and Cancun offer the expected sun and sand, Guanacaste offers much more and includes incredible beaches. An essential feature that makes it so attractive to visitors is the vegetation, climate and fauna. Having those elements close to the beach and the sun makes Guanacaste the destination that has it all, making it an ideal place for almost all tourists.
Lots to do On the other hand, Guanacaste is a varied region with various activities and places to visit. This allows you to carry out multiple recreational activities depending on the area you visit. As a result, you can surf, swim, zipline, hike, kayak, camp, raft, and more.
In contact with nature Guanacaste has beautiful mountains full of exceptional views and landscapes. It has more than 9 protected wild areas that allow you to enjoy nature at its best. This region also offers volcanoes and rivers and the multitude of activities that accompany them. In this way, enjoying nature is an essential part of Guanacaste thanks to its locations and stable climate and temperature.
Exclusivity Guanacaste's location makes it possible to avoid the crowding and high density of tourists that characterize the main tourist places. Furthermore, its tranquil atmosphere provides the ideal setting for family time and a relaxing vacation.
Culture and folklore Staying a little further from the popular areas allows you to experience the unique and charismatic folklore of Guanacaste, with all its dances, music, and delicious cuisine. Adventure Although Guanacaste has a peculiarity of peace and tranquility as its main attractions, it still has many motor sports and outdoor activities for the whole family to enjoy.
Quality and safety of accommodation According to the UCR (University of Costa Rica), Guanacaste offers international hotel chains such as Hilton, Barceló, Marriott, Riu and Sol Meliá. Also, given their exclusivity, the regions lack the dangers of big cities. dental tourism Like other regions of Costa Rica, dental tourism is always present. In addition to experiencing all the characteristics of Guanacaste, you also have the opportunity to have your dental treatment at affordable prices. Today, CR offers multiple dental offices specializing in this type of therapy, creating high-quality, personalized treatment plans while allowing you to enjoy the wonders of Guanacaste.
