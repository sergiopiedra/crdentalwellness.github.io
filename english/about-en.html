"15 years ago I started this great project in Santa Cruz Guanacaste"
Dr. Mario Rodríguez.

Costa Rica Dental Wellness Centre is an initiative of Dr. Mario Rodriguez in 2004. His idea from the beginning was to offer dental services based on high quality standards and affordable prices for most of the inhabitants of the community of Santa Cruz.
Since then, his clinic became an example of service in the area and this is where the people find a prompt and efficient solution to all of their dental problems.
In an effort to continue growing as an innovator in the dental field, Dr.Rodriguez has been seriously pursuing continued education in order to provide advanced and high quality treatments to his patients, supported by the new technology available in the market as digital radiovisiography, that highly reduces the radiation effects on his patients; implementing metal free dentistry, dental implants, intraoral camera, etc.. all to make the dental experience something completely different and rewarding.

The main objective of our clinic has always been to provide the best service to our national and international patients, who over time have acquired better knowledge of the treatments to be performed, giving the patient an excellent result.

For the above mentioned Dr. Mario Rodríguez (founder) and his team (specialists) are always in continuous training and updating with the best technologies offered by the dental world.

We are located 45 minutes from Daniel Oduber International Airport, 40 minutes from Tamarindo Beach, Junquillal Beach, Playa Negra, Playa Grande, Avellanas Beach, etc.
